How LoyalLocal Works

1️⃣

Business Setup

Businesses quickly register and define their simple loyalty program (e.g., "5th visit gets 50% off").

📱

Log Visits

At checkout, businesses enter the customer's phone number to log a visit or purchase. No apps for customers to download!

🎁

Earn & Redeem

The system tracks progress. When a reward is earned, it's flagged for easy redemption on the next visit.

Key Features

📞

Phone Number Based

Uses familiar phone numbers as unique identifiers. No need for physical cards or complex QR codes.

⚙️

Simple for Businesses

Easy setup and an intuitive dashboard for managing customers and tracking loyalty progress.

😃

Frictionless for Customers

Customers don't need to download an app. They just provide their phone number to participate.

📈

Boost Retention

Encourage repeat business by rewarding customer loyalty and making them feel valued.

💬

Optional SMS Alerts

Keep customers engaged with automated SMS notifications for new enrollments and earned rewards (with consent).

📊

Basic Insights

Track key metrics like total customers, visits, and rewards redeemed to see your program's success.
